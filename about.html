    EpicMode

    About Us

    EpicMode is a disruptive project for the Fashion industry. Fashion and Textiles is the world's 2nd largest industry ($2.3T USD per year), 2nd largest polluter, and 1st largest user of child and indentured slave labor. Change will not happen from within in the conservative Fashion industry. New players, processes, workflows, and a grassroots global movement designed specifically for change is required.

    EpicMode enables change by redefining the production process from design through manufacturing. EpicMode positions the fashion industry for success in the Next Economy by utilizing open source code, open data formats, collaborative culture, and 21st Century parametric and 3D design. EpicMode removes barriers to entry, improves efficiency, removes Victorian pseudo-science, and introduces shareable parametric design into the 100% proprietary and secretive Fashion industry.

    Other industries can benefit from EpicMode's 3D design and production features. EpicMode's single solution workflow can create 3D clothing for use in gaming and animation. With EpicMode, you and your gaming character can wear the same clothes and armor!
