    EpicMode

    About Us

    Epicmode is a disruptive project for the Fashion Industry

    Fashion and Textiles is the world's 2nd largest industry ($2.3T USD per year), 2nd largest polluter, and 1st largest user of child and indentured slave labor. Change will not happen from within in the conservative Fashion industry. New players, processes, workflows, and a grassroots global movement designed specifically for change is required.

    EpicMode redefines the process from design through production and publication. EpicMode positions the fashion industry for the Next Economy by utilizing open source code, open data formats, collaborative culture, and parametric design. EpicMode removes barriers to entry, reduces lead times, eliminates Victorian pseudo-science, improves fit and quality, and introduces shareable re-sizable patterns into the 100% proprietary and secretive Fashion industry. EpicMode enables incorporation of circuity, efficient use of new materials, new shapes of clothing. EpicMode maximizes creative possibilities by integrating 2D, 3D, and Virtual Reality features.

    Other industries can benefit from EpicMode's 3D and VR design, production, and publication features. EpicMode's single solution workflow can create digital clothing for use in film, gaming and animation, and online sales. Changes made in Virtual Reality autopopulates to your production pattern and your online catalog or asset library.
