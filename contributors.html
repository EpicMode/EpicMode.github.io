    EpicMode

    Contributors

    EpicMode has contributors and advisors in Ukraine, China, Taiwan, Vietnam, France, Germany, England, and USA. We have presented at conferences and held workshops in Singapore, Hong Kong, Shenzhen, Austria, Belgium, Germany, England, and Canada.

    EpicMode held a design sprint during Libre Graphics Meeting 2016. Programmers from MakeHuman, CNR-ISTI (the developers of MeshLab), and Valentina met to determine the architecture and roadmap for the four main features of EpicMode:

    • 3D Character
    • 3D Measurement
    • 3D Pattern
    • 3D Visualization

    This meeting was made possible through corporate sponsorship for travel, per diem, and accommodations.

    Call for partners

    EpicMode is seeking individual, NGO, corporate, and university partners to develop its 2D and 3D features, design the user interface, create translations, develop international educational materials, and provide travel funding.

    Additional partners

    The Arte Diez charter school in Madrid is developing a Valentina 2D patternmaking manual for use in their design program.
